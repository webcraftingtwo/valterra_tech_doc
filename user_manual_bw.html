01
What Is This App?

The SLAM Re-Entry App is a digital version of the SLAM re-entry procedure you already know from Procedure UNK-MIN-MIN-PRO-0002. It runs on your phone in a browser — no download, no installation.

It walks you through all six phases of the re-entry procedure one step at a time. You cannot skip critical steps. The app automatically checks your gas readings against legal limits, enforces the 2-hour blast lapse, and starts the 30-minute timer when you enter the section.

The app does four things that paper cannot: It calculates the 2-hour lapse automatically. It validates your gas readings against legal limits in real time. It lets your supervisor on surface see your progress live. And it keeps a permanent digital record of every re-entry you do — linked to your badge ID forever.
02
Signing In
1
Open the app URL in your browser
Your supervisor or SHE Manager will give you the URL. Save it to your home screen so you can find it quickly. It works on any Android or iPhone browser.
2
Enter your Badge ID and password
Your Badge ID is your employee number (e.g. VM-00214). Your password was set up during training. If you forget your password, tell your Shift Boss or SHE Manager before your shift — do not wait until you are underground.
3
Check your zone
When you sign in, select your zone from the dropdown at the top of the screen. This is how surface control knows where you are. Do this before you go underground.
If you cannot sign in, do not go underground without completing your paper form. Tell your Shift Boss immediately. Never skip the SLAM procedure because the app is not working.
03
The Six Phases — Step by Step

The app guides you through the same six phases as your paper form. You must complete each phase before the next one unlocks. Steps marked CRITICAL cannot be skipped — the app will block you if you try.

Phase 9.1Re-Entry Preparation

Complete at the Lamp and Crush Building before going underground.

  • Enter the blast time — the app calculates the 2-hour lapse automatically
  • If less than 2 hours have passed, the app shows a countdown and blocks you from proceeding Critical
  • Confirm SCADA fan check, gas clearing, re-entry time on blasting board Critical
  • Enter your confirmed re-entry time
Phase 9.2Waiting Place Management

Complete at the waiting place with your crew assembled.

  • Record your Safety Talk topics by tapping the chips
  • Add each crew member by name and role — tap Add Crew Member Critical
  • Confirm Toolbox Talk sign-in, PPE inspection, fit-for-duty check Critical
Phase 9.3Initial Re-Entry

This is the most critical phase. The 30-minute countdown must be started before you proceed.

  • Confirm NSS box switched to NO BLAST and padlocked Critical
  • Tap START on the 30-minute timer — you cannot advance without starting it Critical
  • Enter your gas readings: CO, NO₂, O₂, H₂S from your detector
  • If any reading fails, the app shows the retake or abort workflow — follow it
  • Set the FOG light status — if RED, the app sends an immediate alert to surface and blocks all further progress Critical
  • Confirm PCU disconnect and barricades installed Critical
Phase 9.4Watering Down & Barring Down
  • Confirm gas test at blasted end, watering down, and barring down per PRO-0003 Critical
  • Enter water blast duration (minimum 15 minutes)
  • Describe ground conditions in the observations field
  • Take photos of bad hangings or hazardous ground — tap Add Photo
Phase 9.5Explosives Search
  • Confirm blown-out explosives search by Blasting Licence holder Critical
  • Record if misfires were found — if yes, complete misfire detail per PRO-0067
  • Record explosives quantities collected and disposed
Sign-OffSupervisor Confirmation
  • Tap the officer sign-off box to confirm your identity
  • Enter supervisor name and tap their sign-off box
  • Add any overall remarks
  • Tap Submit Record — the record is saved and locked permanently
04
Emergency Features
🆘
SOS Button
Always visible — bottom right corner of screen. One tap to open, confirm to send. Notifies surface immediately with your name, zone, and time. Works even if the database is unreachable.
⚠
Near Miss
Tap Near Miss in the toolbar at any time during the procedure. Takes 60 seconds. Saved directly to the safety record system. Far faster than paper.
🔴
FOG Light Red
If FOG light is red, select RED in Phase 3. The app blocks the procedure completely and sends an automatic alert to Rock Engineering and the S-team. Withdraw your crew immediately.
📵
No Signal
The app saves your work locally if there is no signal. An amber bar appears at the top. When signal returns the record syncs automatically. Never lose your progress underground.
05
Common Questions
What if my phone battery dies mid-procedure?
The app saves your progress automatically after every phase. When you charge your phone and reopen the app, your draft is restored from where you left off. You do not lose your work. Your paper form is also running in parallel — so the procedure is covered regardless.
What if I enter the wrong gas reading?
If the reading is outside legal limits, the app will flag it and give you the option to retake. If you entered a value correctly but the area is genuinely unsafe, use the Abort button — this saves the record properly as an aborted re-entry and notifies surface. Do not try to work around an unsafe gas reading.
What if the 2-hour lapse has not passed yet?
The app shows a live countdown and blocks you from advancing to Phase 2. Wait for the countdown to reach zero. This is not a bug — it is the procedure enforcing itself. Use the time to complete your safety talk preparation.
What if I exceed 30 minutes underground?
The timer turns red and shows a warning. Your Shift Boss on the dashboard will also be alerted. Follow the procedure — return to the waiting place immediately if the 30 minutes expires before you are done. Complete the re-entry on the next cycle.
Do I still need to complete the paper form?
Yes — during the pilot period, both are required. The paper form is the official record during the pilot. The digital app is running alongside it. This will be reviewed after the pilot period.
I forgot my password. What do I do?
Tell your Shift Boss or SHE Manager before your shift. They can reset your password from the dashboard and give you a temporary one. Never go underground without completing the SLAM procedure because you cannot access the app.
06
Shift Handover

At the end of your shift, tap the Handover button in the toolbar. Fill in any outstanding hazards, gas concerns, panels to avoid, and general notes for the incoming crew. The incoming Overseer Miner will see your handover automatically when they sign in.

A good handover takes 3 minutes and could save someone's life. Be specific. If there is a bad hanging you could not reach, say exactly where. If a panel has been giving borderline gas readings, say which one. The incoming crew deserves to know what you found.
